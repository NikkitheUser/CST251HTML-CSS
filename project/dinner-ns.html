    Steak and Stewed Tomatoes With a Side of Mashed Potatoes

    Ingredients

    • Steak
    • Tomatoes (canned tomatoes were used)
    • Potatoes
    • A Frying Pan
    • A Pot
    • (optional)
    • Garlic
    • Garlic salt

    How to cook

      This recipe might be harder for some students to pull off, however the results can last you a few days of leftovers. Do note that the mashed potatoes don’t have to be made if you would rather leave them off the menu.

    1. Step 1: First thing you must do is add water to your pot and let the water boil.

    2. Step 2: With your selected meat, start chopping it into small pieces.

    3. Step 3:Now with your chopped up meat, place it into your frying pan and start seasoning it

    4. Step 4: After you’re done seasoning the meat with the seasonings of your choice, you let it cook on the stove top

    5. Step 5: While your steak starts cooking, take the time to start peeling your potatoes and chop them up.

    6. Step 6: once your water boils, add in your potatoes to the mix.

    7. Step 7: Open up your canned tomatoes (or chop them up) and add them to the frying pan with your steak.

    8. Step 8: Now let everything simmer, the potatoes will take approximately 20 minuets to cook.

    9. Step 9: Once the potatoes are done being boiled, drain out the water. Now add in milk and butter to the mix and begin to mash them until you get your desired consistency.
    10. Enjoy
