    Chocolate Brownies

    Ingredients

    • Butter
    • Salt
    • Granulated Sugar
    • 3 eggs
    • Vanilla
    • Flour
    • Cocoa powder (we used Ghiradelli brand)
    • Choclate Chips (optional)

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 300 degrees

    2. Step 2:Prepare baking dish: spray and 8 by 8-inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray, set aside.

    3. Step 3: Melt butter: place butter in a large microwave safe bowl. Heat in microwave until just melted.

    4. Step 4: Mix in sweetener and salt: Stir in sugar and salt, let cool slightly if warm.

    5. Step 5: Mix in wet ingredients: add in eggs and vanilla. Break yolks and stir with wooden spoon just until blended

    6. Step 6: Mix in dry ingredients: add in flour, cocoa powder and chocolate chips. Fold and stir mixture with wooden spoon just until combined. .

    7. Step 7:Add batter to pan: pour into prepared baking dish. Spread into an even layer. Bake in preheated oven just until barely set, about 40 â€“ 46 minutes (toothpick may have a bit of sticky batter, careful not to over-bake).

    8. Step 8:Cool before cutting (if you can wait!): let cool completely on a wire rack then cut into individual squares. Store in an airtight container at room temperature..
